The British School New Delhi is an independent, multicultural, English Medium, co-educational international school. The not-for-profit school offers the National Curriculum of England adapted to an international context, the EYFS programme, the IGCSE and the IB Diploma Programme.[1] With over 1,200 students from 66 nationalities, the school provides student-centred education in a safe, stimulating and multicultural environment.[2] The British School was awarded the Top British International School Award by BISA (British International School Awards) in London in January 2018.[3] They also won the Outstanding Initiative to Support Student Safeguarding award at the same event. In addition, the school was recognised in Fortune India's Future 50 Schools Shaping Success.

    As the Covid-19 outbreak striked, schools were forced to shut down. Therefore, there was no physical school possible. The one and only solution left was ONLINE CLASSES. It took a bit of time for the schools to plan out the way they are going to teach in this online enviroment. Of course when online classes started, everyone was nervous on how they would turn out to be. Although, my school which is The British School, managed this task exceptionaly well. If I have to sum it up, I would say my school has managed online school in a great manner.

Campus Life

school church

Was established in 1978

It is a school church where students are encouraged to go in the break and after school.

school library

Was built in 1964, renovated in 2015

The school library has a variety of books for the children. It is open for children and teachers to come whenever they are free. The lirary also has air-conditioning and a tv for the teachers to make children watch educational movies etc.

school auditorium

Was built in 2004, renovated in 2014

The auditorium is used for house functions, award ceremonies etc. It has a huge capacity of over 500 people.
